Goal: Find the golden treasure by going through the maze or destroying the blocks.

Please use a browser that supports canvas.

FPS: --

Controls: W/S — forward/back   A/D — left/right   Q/E — turn   Mouse drag — look around   Space — jump   R — add block   F — remove block

What You're Looking At:

  1. Canvas: A 3D maze. You move through corridors; brown walls are two blocks tall. The gold block is the goal.
  2. Goal: Find the yellow gold block. Look at it and left-click to win. You can go through the maze or break walls with F.
  3. Controls: W/S/A/D to move, Q/E or mouse drag to turn, Space to jump. R adds a wall in front, F removes one (block by block). Gold cannot be broken.

How to Play:

Move to the gold block, face it, and left-click to show the win screen. Click the overlay to restart.
