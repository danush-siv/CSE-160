A WebGL maze game. Find the golden treasure block by navigating the maze or breaking walls. Left-click the gold (while looking at it) to win.

## Features

- **Maze:** Procedurally generated connected maze with 2-block-wide corridors
- **Walls:** Thin brown walls (two blocks tall); break with F, add with R
- **Gold block:** Unbreakable; look at it and left-click to win
- **Camera:** W/S/A/D move, Q/E turn, mouse drag to look, Space to jump
- **Sky:** Gradient and sun

## How to Use

1. Open `asg3.html` in a modern browser (or use the live demo below)
2. Move with W/S/A/D and turn with Q/E or mouse drag
3. Press R to add a wall in front, F to remove one (block by block)
4. Find the yellow gold block, look at it, and left-click to win
