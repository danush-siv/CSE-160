Far far away, a brave little princess left her castle to find the golden treasure the desert winds had whispered about. The ruins had grown into a twisty maze, and the walls were so shy they sometimes got in the way, but she knew that if she tapped the golden block with her finger, the treasure would be hers at last.

What You're Looking At:

  1. Phong-lit world: The desert maze from ASG3, now illuminated by a moving point light with ambient, diffuse, and specular components.
  2. Spheres & Torus: Two spheres and a spinning torus demonstrate smooth Phong shading on curved geometry (the torus serves as the OBJ-complexity model).
  3. Light controls: Sliders move the point light and change its color. Toggle buttons control lighting, normal visualization, spotlight, and light animation.
  4. Spotlight: A cone light pointing straight down from above, adding focused illumination.
