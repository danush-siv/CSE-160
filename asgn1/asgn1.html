What You're Looking At:

  1. Canvas: The white drawing area on the left where you can create your own artwork.
  2. Danush's Drawing: The image on the right shows a hand-drawn picture of a house with a cloud and a sun that was created on graph paper.
  3. Your Choice: You can try to recreate Danush's drawing (the house with cloud and sun) using the drawing tools, or just have fun creating your own art on the canvas!

How to Draw: Select a shape type (Squares, Triangles, or Circles), adjust the color and size using the sliders, then click and drag on the canvas to draw.
